The Team

  • Dr. Jack Peterson

    Project Lead
    Jack is a physicist and entrepreneur who has successfully led several web and scientific software development projects. He developed deep interests in distributed networks and cryptocurrencies while finishing his PhD at UCSF. Jack has published several peer-reviewed articles on networks and information theory.

  • Joey "Wizard" Krug

    Developer
    Joey recently took a leave of absence from Pomona College (Computer Science) to focus full time on Augur. Joey has spent most of his life honing his programming skills, and has an in-depth knowledge of Bitcoin Core. He conducted the first Bitcoin transaction via sound. Recently, Joey and Jack completed SideCoin, which allows new crypto-currencies distributions to be based on a "snapshot" of Bitcoin account balances.

  • "Duke" Scott Leonard

    Developer
    Scott is a full-stack web developer. He received a degree in Computer Information Systems with an emphasis on Business Administration. With over twenty years experience working in the field -- for large media companies and on small, open-source projects -- he has developed a holistic perspective on systems design and a critical eye for developing technologies.

  • Alan Lu

    Developer
    Alan is a talented programmer, musician, and physics/mathematics prodigy. He is deeply interested in the mathematics behind Augur, and his work is focused on time-lapse cryptography. While new to the world of cryptocurrency, he has a strong knowledge of cryptography. Alan's formidable mathematical skill makes him a major asset to the project.

  • Zack Hess

    Hacker
    Zack is a Python programmer who makes blockchains. He is working on truthcoin to end all governments. Zack wrote Basiccoin, which is the blockchain that the Augur prototype is built upon. He is writing Slasher, which you can read about here and here.

  • Chris Calderon

    Developer
    Chris studied mathematics at Whittier College and specializes in Python. On the Augur project, his focus is on our C++/Bitcoin Core and Ethereum/Counterparty (Serpent) implementations. Chris does creative programming in his free time, with side projects ranging from Python HTTP servers, small 3D libraries, and a program which makes arbitrarily sized PNGs of Mandelbrot fractals.

  • Jeremy Gardner

    Marketing
    Jeremy attended both Bard College and University of Michigan, where developed multidisciplinary studies in political strategy. While at Michigan, he founded the College Cryptocurrency Network, an international nonprofit organization, and began to work with several blockchain-related startups. You can often find him on the road, at a conference or club, extolling the virtues of the blockchain to anybody within earshot.

  • Connor Higgins

    Marketing
    Connor has a degree in business administration, and has been enamored with Bitcoin since he first realized it was more than just magical internet money. He has 7 years experience working on startups, small businesses, and making winning bets.
